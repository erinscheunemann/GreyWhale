Encounter With Whale Watchers

Migrating gray whales can often be seen from the seaside. You can easily learn the technique. Look close to shore and scan your eyes slowly from left to right across the horizon. Watch for anything that breaks the surface of the ocean water. Focus on those objects and look for movement. Then look for spray from the blowhole and splash from tails.

Whale Watching from shore has no effect on migrating whales.
