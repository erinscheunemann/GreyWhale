About Whale Swimming Speeds

Most Migrating gray whales swim 24 hours per day. Although the speed may vary, it falls between 4 and 12 kilometers per hour.

Occasionally a gray whale is observed sleeping near shore, but this is the exception rather than the rule.

In this simulation we are not including the sleeping behavior.

Some whales are faster swimmers than gray whales, others are slower. For example:

Whale Speed (km/hr)
Fin Whale 22-26
Sperm Whale 18-37
Right Whale 3-9
Humpback Whale 10-18
